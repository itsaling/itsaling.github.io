What is it?

As you can already tell, its Aling is a website that I both designed and developed to showcase my portfolio and skill set as a front-end web developer.

I utilized Adobe XD to wireframe, design, and prototype the web site before actually developing the HTML structure because it’s much easier to have a vision and layout before spending time developing it blindly. To build the site I took advantage of libraries such as jquery, and anime.js to add simple animations to the site to make it more interesting to the eyes.

Challenges

Designing the look of the website was the first part and the most challenging part because I wanted the website to reflect my personality while maintaining a clean and modern style. I’ve spent a lot of time creating different versions of the site and experiment with different color schemes as well as look for inspiration in sites like Awwwards, dribble, and Pinterest. Eventually, I landed on this current design to which I feel channels my style and personality.

Another challenge I faced was building the website itself. There were times where I would get stuck with CSS position and making the site responsive. With lots of practice and hours of time spent learning from other sources, I was able to accomplish my vision within the first week of development.

What is it?

Mane Choice, is designed to help users find the right hair products for their particular hair types based on the answers to the quiz questions at the beginning of the app. By utilizing our simple questions, the everyday user can navigate the complex world of hair types with ease.

Our app utilizes series of different activities for each question we ask the user to select. (Total of 6 questions).

One of the challenges my team and I faced was the algorithm for how are we’re going to recommend certain products for each of the answer combinations.

Challenges

For the product recommend, my team host all our data through My Json Server (GitHub Repo), then we make a network call to the the server to retrieve the products.

With the time that my team had, we narrowed down the most important factors that contribute to the type of products that’s going to be recommended (Type, Absorbent, and Fullness). Then for the products, we broke it down to 4 categories which are lightweight, normal, heavyweight and volumizing products.

Another challenge was in the limitations in the amount of data can be hosted using my JSON server. Our data exceeded the 10,000 max contents so we had to host the JSON on two repositories.

What is it?

As fellow pet owners, we all love our pets and would do anything for them. Sadly, on occasion pets have been known to run away, or get lost during terrible natural disasters. In repsonse, we founded a web application that would allow us to connect with those whom have lost or found pets.

Find My Paws is a dynamic web application,functioning as a virtual bulletin board dedicated for users to post information, search for, and find their lost pets.

Challenges

One challenge was face was creating the Login/signup validations. Due to the lack of experience in PHP my team and I had a hard time coming up with how to set the validation of various users via their email and username.
